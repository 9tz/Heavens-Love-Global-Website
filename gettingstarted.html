Discover Salvation

Salvation is at the heart of everything we believe at Heavens Love. Through the boundless grace of Jesus Christ, we are invited into a life-changing relationship with God. By accepting Jesus as our Lord and Savior, we receive the gift of eternal life, finding peace, purpose, and love beyond measure.

If you're ready to start this incredible journey, reach out to one of our pastors. We’d love to welcome you into a new life in Christ.

Embrace Baptism

Serving

Serving in the church is a meaningful way to grow in your faith while contributing to a larger purpose. At Heavens Love, each ministerial arm offers unique opportunities for you to use your gifts, engage with others, and support the work of the ministry.

As you serve, you’ll be part of a community dedicated to fostering spiritual growth, community connection, and the spread of God’s love. Start as an In-Service Steward, supporting our services and learning alongside others in a nurturing environment. As you grow, you’ll have opportunities to take on greater responsibilities within our ministry teams.
