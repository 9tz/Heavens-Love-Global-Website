Our Story

Heavens Love Global was born from a Spirit-inspired calling in late 2020, when Reverend Yiannis Markus felt led to plant a church on the IIEMSA campus in South Africa. What began as a small campus fellowship quickly blossomed into a thriving Christian community, impacting students with the powerful message of Christ. Driven by the Holy Spirit, Heavens Love has since expanded beyond the campus, reaching out to underserved communities like Zandspruit and transforming countless lives.

Today, Heavens Love Global embraces a growing online presence and has extended across borders to multiple campuses, including the University of Botswana and various South African universities. With a vision that’s "too global to be local," the ministry is passionately committed to spreading hope and transformation across nations, fulfilling its divine mission to reach the world with the gospel. Join us as we carry this light forward.

Our Beliefs

Our Mission & Vision

Vision: To spread the gospel of Jesus Christ through the demonstration of God’s power at work in us!

Mission: We aim to be a sanctuary where all people can encounter God’s divine presence, love, and grace. Our doors are open to everyone, regardless of background, welcoming them to experience the transformative power of the Holy Spirit.

Inspired by the Holy Spirit, we are dedicated to embodying and sharing God’s love with a world in need of His touch, inviting all to experience His grace, forgiveness, and life-changing power.

Vision 2027 is a divine mandate given to Reverend Yiannis to see Heavens Love Global expand and impact lives on a global scale. Inspired by the theme of “takeover,” this vision seeks to establish Heavens Love Global on campuses throughout the SADC region and beyond.

With a mission to ignite a movement fueled by the Holy Spirit, we aim to spread the gospel across the nations, working through our dedicated ministerial arms and leaders to accomplish this God-given goal. By 2027, we envision a world touched by the love of Christ, with Heavens Love Global at the forefront of a spiritual revival that transcends borders.
